Why Give

The case for change

Empowering a girl empowers her entire family and community. When women are economically independent, they reinvest up to 90% of their income back into their families.

Through Girls-Coding, Empowered Hands, GirlsInSTEM, and GC Mentors, we help girls achieve economic independence before age 18 — breaking the cycle of poverty for generations.

97% of our donors report trust and confidence in how their contributions are used. We are transparent, mission-driven, and committed to real impact.
