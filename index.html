Digital Skills

Girls Coding

Teaching underserved girls aged 7–17 HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python through Summer Code Camps and AfterSchool programs.

Vocational

Empowered Hands

Seasonal vocational program teaching bead-making, fashion designing, Ankara crafts, hair styling, baking, and wig making.

Innovation

Lady Labs

Providing girls from the poorest communities, CS students, and female tech founders with practical IT skills and co-working space.

Why We Exist

Empowering a girl empowers a community

Through projects like Girls-Coding, Empowered Hands, GirlsInSTEM, and GC Mentors, girls gain functional skills that help them achieve economic independence before age 18. Empowered women transform communities and contribute to economic prosperity.

Our vision: an African world where women are proportionally represented as technical leaders.
