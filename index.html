Why We Exist

Empowering a girl empowers a community

Through projects like Girls-Coding, Empowered Hands, GirlsInSTEM, and GC Mentors, girls gain functional skills that help them achieve economic independence before age 18. Empowered women transform communities and contribute to economic prosperity.

Our vision: an African world where women are proportionally represented as technical leaders.
