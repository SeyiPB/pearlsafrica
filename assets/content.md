2\. About Page

Organizational Identity

* **Entity:** Pearls Africa Foundation (Social enterprise)  
* **Focus:** Advancing young girls and women, especially vulnerable girls in underserved communities.

Detailed Mission

* **Goal 1:** Inspire girls and women to excel in STEM and technology careers.  
* **Goal 2:** Build community of girls and women in STEM+, focusing on ethics, leadership, self-empowerment, confidence, public speaking, self-esteem, and economic independence.

Philosophy

* **Belief 1:** Every girl deserves equal opportunity and empowerment irrespective of background.  
* **Belief 2:** Mentorship by women is vital.  
* **Belief 3:** Girls and women have the value to achieve goals and transform their communities.

3\. Programs Page

Overview

**Description:** The programs are designed to empower girls and women with technical, social, and life skills.

**Program List & Descriptions**

| Program Name | Target Audience/Focus | Skills/Activities | Duration/Note |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Girls Coding** | Underserved girls aged 7–17 | HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python (Tech skills) | Rolling |
| **Safe Space** | Girls & Women (Mentorship) | Personal health, child abuse, career choices, sexual harassment support, cultural practices | 3-month virtual learning community |
| **Ladies at Work** | Undergraduates and graduates seeking employment | Digital skills, job preparation | Monthly Workshops |
| **Empowered Hands** | Vocational training seekers | Bead-making, fashion designing, weaving, Ankara crafts, hair styling, baking, wig making | Seasonal program (July–August) |
| **Lady Labs** | Girls from poorest communities, CS students, female tech founders | Functional IT skills, practical experience, co-working space | Rolling |
| **Community Outreach** | Underserved communities | Feeding programs, orphanage visits, relief material donations, cook-outs | Rolling |
| **Medical Outreach** | Uninsured/medically underserved | Free health care, education on disease prevention | Rolling |
| **Educate Her** | Promising girls from underprivileged communities | Scholarships | Rolling |
| **School Outreach** | Secondary school girls | Career talks and mentorship | Rolling |
| **Internship Placement** | Girls pursuing pre-career work experience | Assists with placement in IT companies | Rolling |
| **Pearls Across Africa** | Community | Builds ecosystem of digitally savvy and financially independent child-rights advocates | Rolling |
