**Section 2 — Why This Work Matters**

Artificial intelligence and digital technologies are transforming industries and redefining the future of work.

Yet women remain significantly underrepresented in technology fields, particularly across developing economies.

Pearls Africa works to ensure African girls and young women gain the skills, mentorship, and opportunities needed to participate in the digital economy.

**Section 4 — Our Approach**

Pearls Africa takes an ecosystem approach to preparing girls and women for the evolving technology landscape. By introducing girls to coding early, building AI awareness, supporting women advancing in technology, and equipping educators, the organization creates a pathway that expands opportunities in the digital economy.

**Pillar 1 — Introduce Girls to Coding**

Through **GirlsCoding**, participants gain early exposure to programming, computational thinking, and digital problem-solving.

**Pillar 2 — Build AI Awareness**

The **Girls in AI** program introduces artificial intelligence concepts and explores how emerging technologies are shaping industries and careers.

**Pillar 3 — Advance Women in AI**

The **Women in AI Initiative** supports women exploring opportunities in artificial intelligence through learning, mentorship, and community building.

**Pillar 4 — Empower Educators**

The **Pearls AI Teachers Fellowship** equips teachers with tools and resources to introduce AI awareness and digital innovation in classrooms.

**Closing Line**

Together, these pillars create a pathway that introduces girls to technology, builds artificial intelligence awareness, supports women advancing in AI careers, and equips educators to guide future innovators.
